MCHREC

A Deckbuilding Recommendation Tool for the Marvel Champions LCG

How It Works

Step 1: Choose your hero by clicking their image

Step 2: Choose your hero's aspect(s)

Step 3: Choose your optional preferences!

  • Step 3a: Choose how many days of deckbuilding history you want to see
  • Step 3b: Select which packs you want to get results from
  • Step 3c: Choose to sort results by synergy or popularity

Step 4: Click the "Get Results" button
